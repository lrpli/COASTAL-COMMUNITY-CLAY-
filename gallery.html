Gallery

Pieces shaped by tide, wind, and fire.

A rotating collection of work by members, residents, and visiting artists made in our Yachats studio.

A selection of ceramic work made in our Yachats studio. To view the full collection in person, visit us daily 10am–6pm or attend one of our seasonal community markets. To submit your studio work for inclusion, email info@coastalclay.us.

Basalt Mug Series

Textured stoneware mugs inspired by the horizontal striations of Yachats shoreline basalt. Carved surface, iron wash, reduction fired.

Stoneware Wheel-thrown Reduction fired

Drift Collection

A series of celadon-glazed bowls with hand-carved wave patterns along the rim. Thrown on the wheel and fired to cone 10 in gas reduction.

Celadon Hand-carved Cone 10

Firelight Vessels

Wood-fired forms from our 2023 Anagama community firing. Natural ash deposits create the distinctive blush and drip surface on each unique vessel.

Wood firing Ash glaze Anagama

Coastal Tableware

Functional dinner sets in warm clay tones with a matte coastal glaze. Designed for everyday use — dishwasher and microwave safe.

Dining set Functional Electric fired

Seafoam Porcelain

Thin-walled porcelain vessels glazed in translucent seafoam tones. High-fire cone 10 reduction brings out the cool mineral quality of the glaze.

Porcelain High fire Gas reduction

Tide Pool Bowls

Nested serving bowls with layered slip decoration and sgraffito line work drawn from coastal tide pool patterns. Each set is hand-made and one of a kind.

Sgraffito Slip work Hand-built
